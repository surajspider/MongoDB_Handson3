Q2. Query the collection "employee" and list all the documents

Answer: To list all the documents - db("Human_Resource").collection("employee").find()

Q3. Query the collection "employee" and list the employees who are having salary more than 30000

Answer: To list the employees having salary more than 30000 - db("Human_Resource").collection("employee").find({salary:{$gt:30000}})

Q4. Query the collection "employee" and list the employees who are having experience more than 2 years.

Answer: To list the employees having experience more than 2 years - db("Human_Resource").collection("employee").find({overallExp:{$gt:2}})

Q5. Query the collection "employee" and list the employees who are graduated after 2015 and having experience more than 1 year

Answer: To list the employees having experience more than 1 yr and graduated after 2015 - db("Human_Resource").collection("employee").find({yearGrad:{$gt:2015},overallExp:{$gt:1}})

Q6. Query the collection "employee" and update the salary of the employee whose salary is greater than 70000 to 65000.

Answer: To Update salary to 65000 from 70000 - db("Human_Resource").collection("employee").updateMany({salary:{$gt: 70000 }},{$set:{salary: 65000 }})

Q7. Delete all the documents from "employee" where last company is Y

Answer: To delete documents of last company is y - db("Human_Resource").collection("employee").deleteMany({lastCompany:"Y"})
